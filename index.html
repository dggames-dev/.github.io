GAME

Viro Busters

A side scrolling shooting game where you pick weapons and shoot to destroy the laboratory and its mutated virus as enemies.

STORY

A Scientist ordered to end the outbreak of mutated enemies by breaking to the base of the enemies' secret lab.

STORY

Using the Scientists' specialized bullets that He himself develop, He's the only one that can destroy them. Inside the lab The Scientist may face its high security defenses and numerous mutated enemies, luckily the lab also had its health medicines and weapons that may aid the Scientist to obliterate his enemies.
